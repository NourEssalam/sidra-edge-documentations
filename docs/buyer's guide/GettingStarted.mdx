# Getting Started with Sidra Edge

## Sign Up

To begin your journey with Sidra Edge, sign up for an account on our website. It's quick, easy, and free!

## Dashboard Overview

Once logged in, you'll be greeted with the dashboard. Here, you can manage your computing resources, deploy projects, and track your activity.

## Set Up Computing Resources

Navigate to the "Computing Resources" section to configure your resources according to your requirements. You can adjust CPU cores, memory, and network bandwidth.

## Deploy Your Project

With your computing resources set up, you're ready to deploy your project. Choose your deployment method and follow the prompts to get your project up and running.

Get ready to witness the power of Sidra Edge in action!
