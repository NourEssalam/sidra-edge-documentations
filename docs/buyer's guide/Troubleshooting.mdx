# Troubleshooting

## Common Issues

Encountering issues with Sidra Edge? Check out these common problems and their solutions:

### Connectivity Issues

- **Problem:** Unable to connect to computing resources.
- **Solution:** Check your network connection and ensure that your resources are properly configured in the dashboard.

### Deployment Errors

- **Problem:** Project deployment fails with an error message.
- **Solution:** Review the error message for details and troubleshoot accordingly. It could be due to incorrect configurations or missing dependencies.

### Performance Degradation

- **Problem:** Project performance is slower than expected.
- **Solution:** Check resource utilization and adjust settings as needed. You may need to allocate additional resources to improve performance.

## Contact Support

If you're unable to resolve an issue on your own, don't hesitate to contact our support team for assistance. We're here to help you get back on track and ensure a smooth experience with Sidra Edge.
