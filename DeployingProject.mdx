# Deploying Your Project

## Upload Your Project

In the "Deployfile" section, you have the option to upload your project directly. Simply select your project files and let Sidra Edge handle the rest.

## Choose a Template

Explore our extensive collection of templates to find the perfect fit for your project. Whether you're building a website, app, or service, we've got you covered.

## GitHub Integration

Connect your GitHub repository to Sidra Edge and deploy your project directly from your repository. It's a seamless process that saves you time and effort.

## Custom Configurations

Want to tweak some settings? No problem! Customize your deployment with options such as domain name, project name, and operating system.

With Sidra Edge, deploying your project has never been easier!
